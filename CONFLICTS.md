# Conflict of Interest Disclosures

NDRIA is neutral. This file records any commercial or material relationships between the
project's maintainers/contributors and the protocols listed in the seed registry or the feed
providers in the feed registry, per the [charter](./CHARTER.md).

## Maintainers

- **Leigh Cronian / Cybercentry** (lead & steward). Cybercentry operates commercial security and
  verification products (token, smart-contract, and wallet verification). For full transparency:
  - Cybercentry has **no commercial relationship** with any of the protocols in the seed
    registry (RFP Section 3) or any feed provider in the feed registry (RFP Section 4).
  - Cybercentry's own products are **not** listed in NDRIA's feed registry and will not be added
    as a privileged or default feed. Should Cybercentry ever be proposed as a feed, it would go
    through the same public PR/rationale process as any other feed and must not be weighted above
    others - consistent with the oracle-diversity principle in [CHARTER.md](./CHARTER.md).
  - NDRIA shows feed data verbatim and produces no risk scores of its own, including none from
    Cybercentry products.
