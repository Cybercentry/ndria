# Stewardship

The RFP requires a named long-term steward - a specific individual or organization with a
public presence and a stated commitment to long-term maintenance - confirmed in writing before
the final payment (M2).

    [ERC-8126](https://eips.ethereum.org/EIPS/eip-8126),
    [ERC-8196](https://eips.ethereum.org/EIPS/eip-8196)
- **Commitment:** Cybercentry commits to maintaining NDRIA as a neutral public good beyond the
  grant period - verifying coverage cells, reviewing community corrections, and keeping the
  data layer current.

## Why this steward is incentivized

Cybercentry's mission is Ethereum-ecosystem security and verification infrastructure. A neutral,
public risk-intelligence aggregator is directly adjacent to that mission and reinforces
Cybercentry's standing as a trusted, neutral security layer for the ecosystem. The steward has a
durable reputational and strategic incentive to keep NDRIA accurate, current, and independent.

## Maintenance model

- **Data verification:** scheduled re-verification of coverage cells; automated refresh for
  machine-readable feeds (M2), manual review for the rest.
- **Community corrections:** PR-based, source-verified before merge (see
  [CONTRIBUTING.md](./CONTRIBUTING.md)).
- **Infrastructure:** hosted under Cybercentry's infrastructure; low fixed cost (static + edge
  functions for live TVL).
- **Neutrality:** maintained per [CHARTER.md](./CHARTER.md) and disclosed in
  [CONFLICTS.md](./CONFLICTS.md).
